Our Shop

We are located on Pennsylvania Route 340 in Intercourse, on the west side of town, facing the traffic light on 772. Click here for more detailed directions.

We are in the heart of Lancaster County's Amish Country.

We carry locally made quilts, comforters, throws, wagons, furniture, scooters, metal crafts, croquet sets, and custom made hardwood cabinets, quilt racks, jewelry chests, and more.

We also carry : Australian Hats, & Coats; handmade turkey, duck, goose, deer, elk, coyote, and predator calls; high quality wooden toys, walking sticks, handbags, handmade baskets, handmade rocking chairs, and many other interesting items...........

Many of our items are made on area Amish farms. We ship worldwide.
