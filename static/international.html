A Note to Our International Customers

Our website software, and apparently the interface to UPS and the Post Office do not deal well with international shipments. Further, our credit card processing account will not honor international bankcards, unless we change our security settings.

If you wish to place an order, please either:

 

Email us with your request, and we will calculate the shipping and handle the transaction by phone or email.

Place the order on the site and choose the "pay by check" option. We will calculate the correct freight and let you know.

If you came here for scooters ....

Just enter the store and you will see the link to the scooter section.
